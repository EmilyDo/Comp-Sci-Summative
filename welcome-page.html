Math Help Program

Having problems in math? The Math Help Program is available to all OCDSB students in grades 7-12. This program is run on a drop in basis and serves as "Homework Help" that provide students with a supervised opportunity to complete, review, practice and organize their work in order to help them achieve greater success at school. It can also assist students in moving toward provincial standards in Mathematics. There is no charge for these programs, however, students are asked to bring along their Ontario Education Number (OEN), which can be found on the top of any Ontario Report Card.

Free tutoring is available every Monday and Wednesday from 3:30PM - 5:30PM in room 168 (first floor) at Earl of March Secondary School (4 The Parkway, Kanata).
